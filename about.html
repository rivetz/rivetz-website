About Rivetz

Pioneering the future of digital relationships with verifiable trust and provable cybersecurity

Our Vision

We envision a digital future built on verifiable trust, where every interaction has provable origin and integrity. We're moving beyond physical relationships with digital infrastructure to create true digital relationships with digital infrastructure.

Rivetz is leading the transition from reactive cybersecurity to proactive data integrity, where data, systems, and services have original, verifiable information that can be validated mathematically, not just assumed based on brand reputation.

Our Journey

Founded in 2013

Launched with a vision to revolutionize trusted computing and blockchain security innovation.

2013

DOD Contracts & Samsung Partnership

Delivered over $1 million in US Department of Defense contract work for mobile security and built amazing technology for Android TEE with Samsung and Trustonic.

2014-2016

Pioneering Token Sale

Executed an ICO in summer 2017, raising $18 million in RvT tokens, with 1/3 of sales in the USA.

2017

Telefonica Partnership

Partnered with Telefonica to develop and patent dual roots of trust technology.

2018

Legal Clarity & Relaunch

Successfully defended RvT token status in court. Token confirmed not a security, only the sale was deemed an investment contract. Company relaunching with renewed purpose for Digital 2.0.

2025
