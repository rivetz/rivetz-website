The Technology Behind Digital 2.0

Combining trusted computing, blockchain, and cryptographic proof to establish a provable, tamper-proof framework for digital interactions

Global Attestation and Identity Network (GAIN)

A transformative approach that combines trusted computing, blockchain technology, and tokenization to establish a provable, tamper-proof framework for devices and digital interactions.

🔍

Provable Device Origin

Every device in the GAIN ecosystem is uniquely identifiable, ensuring that only known and authorized systems can participate in digital transactions.

  • Hardware-based cryptographic attestation
  • Strong digital identifiers
  • Unparalleled verification confidence
⚖️

Decentralized Compliance Automation

Automates compliance by recording validated controls and verifications immutably on the blockchain. Devices are continuously monitored and attested, proving that policies and controls are being enforced at all times.

  • Immutable compliance records
  • Real-time policy enforcement
  • Automated validation processes
